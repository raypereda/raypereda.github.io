Patterns in the Mueller Report

April 22, 2019 · 92 words · Ray Pereda

Let’s find the repeated phrases in the Mueller Report. Here’s the plain text of the report. It is 442 pages. Here’s a PDF of the of output MegaPatterns.com without any preprocessing.

There is lots of noise, like long sequences of periods.

But browsing the lightly processed patterns, I see strong signals. This phrase is repeated 6 times in the Mueller Report:

that it could assist the Campaign through the anonymous release of information

There is no evidence discovered that anonymous releases occurred.

That phrase nicely sums up what is being searched for.
